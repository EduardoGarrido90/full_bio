Full Bio-inspired Hyper Heuristic Bayesian Optimization Search
===============================================================

Apply SMAC to optimize the chosen metaheuristic for a given problem (use PyGMO):

Use:

(Particle Swarm Optimization) PyGMO.algorithm.pso, 
(Simple Genetic Algorithm) PyGMO.algorithm.sga
(Artificial Bee Colony (ABC)) PyGMO.algorithm.bee_colony
(Differential Evolution (DE)) PyGMO.algorithm.de

And their space that optimize the parameters of the food problems.

Also optimize two or three synthetic and benchmark problems.

Tareas:

(4h) 1. Definir espacio de busqueda al estilo SMAC3 de todos los algoritmos propuestos mas busqueda final global.
(4h) 2. Definir metaheuristicas individuales contando busqueda final global.
(4h) 3. Definir wrappers.
(3h) 4. Problemas de prueba. 
(3h) 5. Problemas de la comida.
(4h) 6. Recopilar resultados.
(8h) 7. Escribir paper.

Total: 30h.
